About

Hello—I’m Huang-Cheng (David) Chou, a Postdoctoral Scholar–NSTC Fellow at the Signal Analysis and Interpretation Laboratory (SAIL) led by Prof. Shrikanth S. Narayanan at the University of Southern California.

I earned my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from National Tsing Hua University under Prof. Chi-Chun Lee, specializing in affective computing, speech, and behavioral signal processing. My research focuses on speech emotion recognition and multimodal understanding and perception, with collaborations across the Speech Processing and Machine Learning Lab (Prof. Hung-yi Lee) and the Multimodal Signal Processing Laboratory (Prof. Carlos Busso).

I also bring hands-on experience from Amazon Alexa, ITRI, and RealTek—working on speech and ML systems from research through deployment. I’m motivated by building intelligent, expressive agents that integrate speech, language, and emotion for natural real-world interaction.
